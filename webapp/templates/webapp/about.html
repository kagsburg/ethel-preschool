About Us

Welcome to Ethel Kindergarten

Ethel Kindergarten started in 2020 as a Kindergarten with about 20 (thirty) children and 3 (three) staff Members. In a three bed roomed house with 30 chairs and no table with personal savings of about UgShs 1,000,000. (one million). We have now grown into one of the biggest and best education institution with over 350 members of staff and about 4000 pupils.

Ethel Kindergarten is a God fearing institution that is dedicated to train a wholesome person. The Institution provides a range of services including Daycare, Kindergarten, and transportation of pupils.
